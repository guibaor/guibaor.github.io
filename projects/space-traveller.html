Space traveller on my desk - Short film

Teamwork final project of Animation subject (Year 3 Multimedia Engineering) at ETSE (Universitat de València).

This animation film was fully created using Blender 2.8, the goal of the project was integrating several animation/VFX techniques:

  • Motion capture: Movements recorded at the university MOCAP room and applied to the robot model
  • Rigging and animation: The wooden dummy model was rigged and animated.
  • Motion tracking, compositing and lighting: Used to softly integrate the CGI elements on the original desktop video (Rubik's cube, pen cap and the wooden dummy screen reflection).

Authors: Darío Rodríguez Hernández, Ana Gloria Gálvez Mellado, Bekzat Mukhamedali and Guillem Barceló Orts .
