Particle Collisions & Data Structures

Interactive simulation of particle collision inside a container, using different collision models and data structures.

At the beginning, a bunch of particles start falling, due to gravity acceleration and can collide with the planes of the container and with each other. The two collision models implemented are: the velocity-based collision and the spring-based collision. To reduce the computation time, two data structures (grid and hash) have been implemented to speed up the calculations of the particle collisions.

Information related to the computation (computation time per frame, FPS, etc.) is displayed on the screen, as well as the different actions that can be executed through keyboard and the mouse: reset the particles, add new particles by clicking or changing between the different collision models and data structures.
